The French Fire is a wildfire buring in the mountains of the Kern River Valley between Keyesville, Wofford Heights, and Alta Sierra. It began at around 4:30 PM on Aug. 18, 2021.

The French Fire has devastated the rural community of the Kern River Valley. It has burned over 20,000 acres, destroyed at least 9 homes as well as several other structures.
